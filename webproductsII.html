Your Commission

Commissioning a website is a collaborative, exciting project. Your site defines what people think about when they remember your company. By working out exactly what you want as you begin and refining it throughout the process, you can ensure that the project reaches its maximum potential. Whether you know a little or a lot about coding, here are some things to think about before you start:

  • Do I know who in my company will oversee the progress of the website, answering questions and giving feedback?
  • Have I looked at the websites of similar companies to identify their strengths and weaknesses?
  • Who do I want to visit and use the website?
  • What are the three most important things I want a visitor to know about the company?
  • What is the one function I would want implemented most urgently?
  • Which function could I live without for a while and be happy to see implemented later?
  • Which brand materials and style guides do I already use?
