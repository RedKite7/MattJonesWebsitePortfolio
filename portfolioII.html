A Dandelion Web Project

Our Brief

Realize Social are a start-up social-media marketing agency, based in the South East of England.


They wanted a minimalist, one-page site which:

  • Clearly displayed their brand and product packages
  • Was perfectly responsive
  • Directed users easily towards contacting the business for a more personal service.

They were also looking for email and print marketing templates which used the same brand materials.
